Vernacular

Vernacular is a quarterly publication featuring multidisciplinary artistic expression, including but not limited to painting, photography, mixed media, performance videos, short film, and music. This is our common language, our vernacular.

In conceiving this magazine, our goal was to create a platform to showcase the talent of artists whose work speaks to us. We have constructed a vessel which allows their creations to be introduced to a larger audience. Although the current circumstances force us to be apart, this is a moment in which artistic expression can facilitate connection.

In the next issues, we are excited to continue to provide a forum for inclusive artistic expression, and to support the arts by way of increasing visibility for our contributors.
